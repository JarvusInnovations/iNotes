iNotes

This app provides novice and experienced listeners alike the opportunity to learn more about the music while listening to a live performance.

Follow along during the performance with real-time slides. Slides automatically advance with the music, pointing out key highlights, engaging details and images relating to the composition.

Access additional supplementary content atany time including a digital version of the program notes, a musical glossary and information about the venue or performing group.

With iNotes you can:

  • Follow along during the performance with real-time program notes including text and translations for vocal works.
  • Watch the slides of information automatically advance with the music on your phoneâ€™s screen during the concert.
  • Enjoy key highlights, engaging details, and images relating to the composition.
  • Access a musical glossary, a digital version of the traditional program notes, and information about venue or group.
  • Adjust the font size and brightness for optimal viewing during a performance in Verizon Hall.

iNotes has been developed to have minimal impact on concertgoers in the hall and has been thoroughly tested in rehearsals and postlude performances. The application is designed with grey text on a black background specifically to minimize light and disruption. The content is custom designed for each piece to optimize the experience of hearing the work without distraction.
